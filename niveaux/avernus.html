 "Avernus (formerly known as The Inferno) is a 2.1 Extreme Demon mega-collaboration level hosted by BoBoBoBoBoBoBo (commonly shortened to Bo) and PockeWindfish and co-hosted by Kyhros. It was published by PockeWindfish and verified by Zoink on 22 March 2023 after 27,732 attempts.[1] The level is notorious for its intense difficulty, primarily from fast-paced wave segments, constant game-mode switching, and precise timings."
