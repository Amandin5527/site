 "Glowy is a 2.0/2.1 Extreme Demon collaboration created by Rob Buck and Galzo, published by Rob Buck, and verified by BoldStep. Its difficulty is mainly achieved through its length, being three minutes long, and the increasingly glowing effects through the level.
